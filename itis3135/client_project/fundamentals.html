Scrum Master Fundamentals

In this page, you will learn more information about the role of a Scrum Master and the different aspects about it, such as what responsibilities does a Scrum Master have and what kinds of skills a Scrum Master should have to be successful.

More Details About Scrum Master:

A Scrum Master is similar to a sports coach. They don't play in the game but they make sure their team know the guidelines and ensures they communicate with each other. They guide the team to ensure that there is victory. A Scrum Master does this by ensuring that the team follows Scrum rules, stays focuses on the goal, and makes sure there are no distractions that get in the way of the team. The lead all of the Scrum events and tracks the progress of the developers.

Responsibilities:

  • Coaching: A Scrum Master constanly teaches the team about Scrum guidelines to ensure they are following the agile framework. They answer all the questions that the team has about Scrum. If a Scrum Master notices there is room for improvement, they will notify the team and provide feeback.
  • Faciliating: Scrum Masters facilate most of the Scrum Ceremonies, whenver it is getting the updates of the developers of the daily standup, or providing feedback to the sprint retrospective. They are always to be the host of those meetings.
  • Removing Obstacles: If there any impediments that block the team from working, then the duty of the Scrum Master is to resolve this impediment. Like a Coach or Sports team, it is to make the job of the developer easier so they do not face any blockers.
  • Team Organization: The Scrum Master ensures that the Scrum board is organized. A Scrum board shows the status of the work that is planned of the sprint. The Scrum Master ask the three questions to the developers in the daily standup. The three questions are: What did you do yesterday? What will you do today? and, Do you have any blockers? Scrum boards used to be on white boards that the Scrum Master would draw on to keep track of the work. Now, there are tools such as JIRA and Azure DevOps that organize the developers work.

Skills:

  • Communication: Communication is key. Scrum Masters has meetings throughout the whole day. They must be able to explain concepts clearly and listen to the team members. Scrum Masters also need to create a safe and healthy environment with the team.
  • Problem Solving: One of the main goals of a Scrum Master is to remove impediments for their developers. They also must be able to identify any blockers and remove them as soon as possible. They also must be able to find any gaps and help the team to fix them.
  • Adaptability: The goal of a successful Scrum team is to continuously improve. To find improvements, teams will do trials and errors to find what is best for them. If something goes well, the team will stick with it. If something fails, the team must adapts and the Scrum Master takes lead and finding a way for the team to change.
  • Leadership: The Scrum Master is a servent leader. They take lead by coaching the developers Scrum and taking accountability for the team.
