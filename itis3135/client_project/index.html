What is Scrum and What is a Scrum Master?

Scrum is an agile framework that helps out with collaborating with teams to deliver brief cycles called sprints. It deals with teamwork, flexibility, and focuses on improving continuously. Team members have their roles, meetings, and understand what their goals are.

A Scrum Master is a leader who coaches the team to follow Scrum pratices, removes impediments, and guides the team to prove constantly.
